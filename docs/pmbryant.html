Let Yourself Go B and B

This site houses the archives of my Typepad blogs, Let Yourself Go... To Old Hollywood and B and B. The text, images and styles have been preserved, while most outgoing links to Typepad domains are disabled, as who knows who will end up owning them in the future. Commenting, search and subscribe buttons are hence visible, but not functional. Links to open the inline images in a new tab do not yet work.

For twenty-one years, off-and-on, I have had a blogging presence on Typepad. From 2004 thru 2012, I wrote about politics, science, and some personal items on B and B. In 2017, I started a classic Hollywood blog called Let Yourself Go... To Old Hollywood, and kept it active until 2025.

Prompted by Typepad's shutdown at the end of September 2025, I have taken charge of all my writings and archived both blogs to this new personal domain. Soon I hope to start a new blog on this same domain. It should be a continuation of Let Yourself Go.
